Build a polished, modern personal budget web app for a couple called Couple Budget Dashboard. Make it feel like a real product people would actually use every week, not a basic CRUD demo. The app should help two partners manage shared household finances plus their own personal spending, with a clean dashboard, interactive charts, strong filtering, and a mobile-friendly experience. Product goal The app should help a couple: Track monthly income, expenses, bills, subscriptions, savings, and goals. Separate shared expenses from personal expenses. See who paid for what. Compare spending by partner. Stay on budget by category. Track recurring bills and savings goals. Understand spending trends over time. User model Two users only by default: Partner A and Partner B, with editable names.
